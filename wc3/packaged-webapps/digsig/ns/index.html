Widgets Digital Signature Namespace

The namespace name http://www.w3.org/ns/widgets-digsig is intended for use with the Widgets 1.0: Digital Signatures specification.

Implementers should be aware that this document is not stable.

The following semantics are assigned to fragments of this namespace.

http://www.w3.org/ns/widgets-digsig#profile
....
http://www.w3.org/ns/widgets-digsig#role-author
This represents the digital signature of the author of the widget package.
http://www.w3.org/ns/widgets-digsig#role-distributor
This represents the digital signature of a distributor of the widget package.
